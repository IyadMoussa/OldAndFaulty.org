The Risks

The risks of a old, or faulty Air Conditioner can be very major! It is needed to be dealt with as soon as possible.

Headaches:

Because of old A.C., headaches is the common. Because of things like Poor Air Quality, Dry Air, Temperature Fluctuations. Poor Air Quality: Old air conditioners may accumulate dust, mold, and other contaminants over time, especially if they are not properly maintained. Breathing in these particles can irritate the respiratory system and lead to headaches. Dry Air: Air conditioners, especially older models, can remove moisture from the air, leading to dry indoor air. This dryness can cause dehydration, which may manifest as headaches in some individuals. Temparature Fluctuations: Older air conditioners may have less precise temperature control or may cool the air too rapidly, causing temperature fluctuations in the environment. Sudden changes in temperature can trigger headaches in some people. -headaches from old air conditioners can be caused by factors such as poor air quality, dry air, and temperature fluctuations. The cooling process primarily involves convection and conduction, with a small contribution from radiation. Therefore, headaches from old air conditioners can be attributed to factors related to convection (e.g., poor air quality, temperature fluctuations) and conduction (e.g., dry air causing dehydration), rather than radiation.
