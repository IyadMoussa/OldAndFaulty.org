Dishwasher

The Dishwasher

The Dishwasher, one of the popular household appliances because it makes your life easier!

The Inner-workings

The Dishwasher uses Convection to use the process of drying the dishes after a wash.

The Risks

Old: Rust might start to appear in the interior of the Dishwasher and won't be able to wash your dishes well.

Faulty: If the Dishwasher's rubber seals dry out and crack, the Dishwasher will start leaking water.

Other Facts

1. The Dishwasher could give you some chronic health issues overtime if you are exposed to the fumes of it for too long.

2. Mold, mildew, or other bacteria can start growing in the Dishwasher. Make sure to check the Dishwasher for bacteria every once and a while!
